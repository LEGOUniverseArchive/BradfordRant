HOW TO FIND A POD

  • The pods are attracted to large amounts of LEGO® bricks and/or imagination
  • Once we know the exact location of the pod we will tell persons at the location to secure the pod
  • If you arrive at a pod location, look for the pod contact person
  • When you find the pod contact person, tell them the number of the pod you seek, e.g. 'Pod No.2'. Make sure you write down the Pod ID.
  • The first person to arrive at the pod location and tell the pod contact person the correct pod number will get to keep the pod
  • Click here for the full Terms and Conditions applicable to this Event.
  • It is still very important for others to make their way to the pod location and write down the Pod ID
  • If you can, take photos and video footage and send it to us here at Bradford Rant bradfordrant@gmail.com. Your data may help others in their search
  • REMEMBER: Always write down the Pod ID!
